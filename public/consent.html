What This Policy Covers

This policy explains how Airosofts LLC will communicate with you after you request a free website demo through our online form. By submitting your contact information, you are giving us permission to send you text messages and emails related to your demo request.

Text Messages (SMS) You Will Receive

We will send you text messages to the phone number you provide. Here's exactly what to expect:

  1. Confirmation Message - Sent within 30 minutes of your request to confirm we received it.
  2. Demo Ready Notification - Sent when your website demo is completed (usually within 24 hours).
  3. Demo Link - A text message containing the secure link to view your personalized website demo.
  4. Follow-up Messages - Up to 2 additional messages over the next 30 days asking if you'd like to turn your demo into a real website.

Total Messages: You will receive approximately 3-5 text messages total.

Important: Message and data rates may apply. These messages are sent using automated technology. You can opt out at any time by replying "STOP" to any message.

Emails You Will Receive

We will send emails to the email address you provide:

  1. Welcome Email - Confirms your demo request and provides a reference number.
  2. Demo Instructions - Detailed instructions on how to view and navigate your demo.
  3. Demo Link Email - The same demo link sent via text, but with additional details.
  4. Feedback Request - One email asking for your thoughts on the demo (optional response).
  5. Service Information - Up to 2 emails over 60 days with information about our website development services.

Total Emails: You will receive approximately 4-6 emails total.

How to Stop Communications

You can stop receiving messages from us at any time:

To Stop Text Messages:

  • Reply "STOP" to any text message from us
  • Send an email to info@airosofts.com with your phone number
  • Call us at (332) 333-3839

To Stop Emails:

  • Click the "Unsubscribe" link at the bottom of any email
  • Send an email to info@airosofts.com
  • Reply to any email with the word "UNSUBSCRIBE"
Note: If you opt out, you will no longer receive your demo link or any updates about your demo. However, you can still contact our customer service team if you change your mind.

Your Information and Privacy

We collect your business name, email address, and phone number solely for the purpose of creating and delivering your free website demo. We do not sell, rent, or share your information with any third parties for marketing purposes.

Your information is stored securely and used only by Airosofts LLC employees who need access to fulfill your demo request.
